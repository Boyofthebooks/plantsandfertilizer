Aim of the project

To know if using fertilizer actually helps boost plant growth

Hypothesis

If a plant is given fertilizer to boost its growth then it will grow faster than a plant without compost

Materials

➢Seeds

➢Soil

➢Homemade Fertilizer

Process

Step 1: Segregate 2 pots with the same seed and same soil type

Step 2:Add fertilizer/compost to one of them and keep them in a place with good sunlight

Step 3:water daily with the same amount of water

Step 4:Measure time taken for germination

Step 5:Leave for 2 weeks and see which grew more
